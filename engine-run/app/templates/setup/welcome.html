Welcome to Plexify

Plexify backs up your Spotify playlists and liked songs and mirrors them into Plex as lossless FLAC — fetching anything missing from Soulseek, SpotiFLAC and more.

You'll connect a few things: Spotify (the source), your Plex server (the destination), and the download apps. Host paths — your music folder and downloads — come from .env.
