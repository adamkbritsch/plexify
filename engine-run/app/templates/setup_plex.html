How to find your Plex token (3 ways, pick whichever is easiest)
  1. Easiest — XML method: Open any track in Plex web. Click the … menu → Get Info → View XML. In the address bar of the new tab, find X-Plex-Token= followed by your token. Copy everything after the equals sign up to the next &.
  2. Account page: app.plex.tv → Settings → Account. Look for "Authorized Devices" → click an entry → token appears in the URL query string.
  3. Plex docs: support.plex.tv guide — has screenshots.

The token looks like a 20-character random string. Don't share it publicly — it grants full access to your Plex server.
