Your Spotify playlists

Every playlist you own on Spotify is auto-backed up as soon as Spotify is connected. The compile runs in the background and resumes after restarts — no buttons to click. Live progress is on the dashboard and jobs page. Enable Plex/Plex mirroring per-playlist from the dashboard toggles.

Playlists you don't own can't be compiled. Since early 2025, Spotify blocks third-party apps from reading the contents of any playlist your account doesn't own — playlists you merely follow as well as algorithmic ones (Daily Mix, Discover Weekly, Wrapped…) — so only playlists you own are listed here at all.
Workaround: in the Spotify app, select all tracks of a followed playlist and add them to a playlist you own — that copy backs up and syncs like any other.
